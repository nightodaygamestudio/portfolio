Who am I

I am Jan, also known as JJampion (/ ˈdʒeɪˈtʃæm.pi.ən /). I am 26 years old and come from Franconia, Germany. Since my childhood - actually as long as I can remember - I have been a passionate video game fan and fascinated by the gaming world. In 2018, I discovered how to root Android systems, which inspired me to delve into reverse engineering of games and manipulation of game values.



A friend of mine showed me the game "Flappy Bird" in 2014-2015, which rekindled my interest years later and led me to create my own version of the game called "New Super Bullet Bill". It's a fusion of Flappy Bird and New Super Mario Bros for the Nintendo DS, one of my favorite games. At first, I just swapped out the sprite sheets, sounds, etc., but eventually it became a full-fledged fan project in GameMaker, with a "villain" as the protagonist. "Fun fact" that I'm not proud of, but I was so addicted to NSMB that as a child I even took my Nintendo DS with me to church, where I had to go to continue playing because I was so hyped when I found out there was a secret world 4 and 7 and how to reach them.



At that time, I decided to pursue my dream of starting my own indie game studio. The name "Nightoday Game Studio" came to me easily, inspired by my mother, who often reminded me not to stay up too late, take care of my eyes, and get enough sleep. and the the legendary Bethesda Game Studios with Skyrim saved me when I was in a children's home and only had one hour of "media time" per day. I invested almost every single day of that hour in this masterpiece. The term "Nightoday" comes from the direct translation "night to day". Thus, the name of my dream studio was born. "Funny fact" 2.0, I have a tattoo in Dovahzul on my right forearm.



My goal is to gain as much experience as possible to start my career as a lead game designer or game director in my own studio. I am determined to translate my passion for games into creative and innovative projects and develop games that will excite and captivate players.



In my portfolio, I will present my previous experiences, projects, and ideas. I have invested a lot of time and effort to improve my skills in game design and development, and I am ready to use my skills and knowledge in my future job as a game designer. I hope that my work in this portfolio will demonstrate my enthusiasm for games and my ability to solve complex problems and find creative solutions.



Thank you for your interest in my portfolio. If you have any questions or are interested in collaborating, please feel free to contact me.
